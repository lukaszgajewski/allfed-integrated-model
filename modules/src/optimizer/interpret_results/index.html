Interpreter

source

This class is used to convert between optimization results data and other useful ways of interpreting the results, as a diet, or as a total food supply.

.interpret_results

source

.interpret_results(
   extracted_results
)

This function takes the raw output of the optimizer food categories and total people fed in list form, and converts the naive people fed which includes negative feed, into a purely list of values, where the negative feed has been subtracted from the sum of outdoor growing and stored food.

ANYTHING assigned to "self" here is part of a useful result that will either be printed or plotted as a result

.get_increased_excess_to_feed

source

.get_increased_excess_to_feed(
   feed_delay, percent_fed
)

when calculating the excess calories, the amount of human edible feed used can't be more than the excess calories. Because the baseline feed usage is higher than in nuclear winter, we don't want to increase feed usage before the shutoff.

this function adds an additional amount of excess at a consistent percentage in the months of interest (months to calculate diet)
